What To Expect

Real moments, relaxed direction, and images that feel like you.

I care about photos that feel honest. The kind that bring you right back to the feeling of a moment instead of just showing what it looked like.

I’ll guide you when you need it, give you space when something real is unfolding, and help the experience feel easy instead of stiff or overly posed.

If you want photos that feel warm, emotional, creative, and completely personal, we’re probably going to make something really beautiful together.

Let’s Talk
Questions & Answers Questions & Answers Questions & Answers

Where are you based and do you travel?

01

I’m based in Southern California and available for weddings, portraits, creative sessions, and travel. If your idea takes us somewhere beautiful, meaningful, weird, fun, or totally personal, I’m in.

What if I feel awkward in front of the camera?

02

You do not have to know what to do. That’s my job. I’ll guide you with movement, prompts, and direction so it feels natural instead of forced. Most people show up nervous and leave saying it was way easier than they expected.

What kind of sessions do you photograph?

03

Weddings, couples, portraits, seniors, musicians, creatives, brands, and storytelling sessions. If it has emotion, personality, or a cool concept behind it, I want to photograph it.

How involved are you in the planning?

04

I’m as involved as you need me to be. I can help with location ideas, timeline flow, outfit direction, moodboards, and creative concepts so the final images feel intentional without making the process stressful.

The Process
